# 输出示例

## 1. Reply Strategy

语气建议友好但谨慎。回复中应确认 BFI 会配合 review，但不要承诺本周五一定给最终结论，也不要接受任何费用、工期或责任影响。

## 2. English Email Draft

Subject: Revised Bracket Detail Review

Hi [Name],

Thank you for your email.

We acknowledge the revised bracket detail and will review it with our factory team against the latest shop drawings and relevant site gridline information.

At this stage, we are not in a position to confirm final acceptance or any programme / cost impact until the review is completed. We would appreciate it if you could provide the latest marked-up drawing reference and the affected locations so we can assess this properly.

We will revert once we have completed the internal review.

Kind regards,  
[Your Name]

## 3. 中文说明

- “will review it with our factory team” 表示配合推进。
- “not in a position to confirm final acceptance” 保留技术结论边界。
- “programme / cost impact” 明确不提前接受工期和费用责任。
