# 输出示例

## 1. Communication Type

Consultant technical comment / Builder instruction with potential VO and programme pressure.

## 2. Core Intent

Builder 希望 BFI 立即接受 revised sill detail，并同时确认无费用和工期影响。隐含压力是把 consultant-driven change 转化为 BFI 的无条件配合义务。

## 3. Key Facts Confirmed by the Text

- **Fact 1**: Consultant does not accept the previous sill detail.
- **Fact 2**: Builder asks BFI to incorporate the revised detail immediately.
- **Fact 3**: Builder asks BFI to confirm no delay and no additional cost.

## 6. Commercial / Contractual Risk

存在 VO、programme impact 和 scope boundary 风险。BFI 不应在技术和工厂 review 完成前确认无费用或无工期影响。

## 10. Suggested Next Steps

1. BFI project manager should request the marked-up detail, drawing revision and consultant comment record.
2. BFI design/factory team should review whether the revised sill detail affects extrusion, waterproofing and procurement.
3. BFI should reply that cost and programme impact are subject to review and cannot be confirmed at this stage.
